FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about Radiance Healthcare.

Home›FAQ

What products does Radiance Healthcare offer?

Radiance Healthcare supplies diagnostic reagents and laboratory instruments for Biochemistry, Hematology, Immunoassay, and other clinical laboratory applications — including fully automated and semi-automated analyzers, reagents, calibrators, controls, and consumables.

When was Radiance Healthcare established?

Radiance Healthcare was established in 2015 and has been serving diagnostic laboratories, hospitals, and healthcare institutions across India ever since.

What makes Radiance Healthcare different?

Radiance Healthcare is led by Mr. Chinmay De, who has over 30 years of experience in the diagnostics industry. Our expertise, high-quality product portfolio, and genuine customer-focused service make us a trusted partner for laboratories across India.

Do you assist with complete new laboratory setup?

Yes. We are specialists in complete laboratory setup — from planning and equipment selection to installation, validation, and staff training. We guide you through every step to get your lab fully operational.

Do you provide installation and training support?

Yes. We provide professional installation assistance, user training, and technical guidance to ensure smooth operation of all laboratory instruments we supply.

Do you offer after-sales service?

Yes. We provide prompt after-sales support, preventive maintenance, troubleshooting assistance, and coordination with manufacturers whenever required.

Which types of customers do you serve?

We serve diagnostic laboratories, hospitals, nursing homes, pathology centers, research institutions, and healthcare organizations of all sizes across India.

Can you help us select the right instrument for our lab?

Absolutely. Our experienced team can recommend the most suitable instruments and reagents based on your testing volume, budget, test menu requirements, and laboratory setup.

Do you supply consumables and reagents?

Yes. We supply a wide range of diagnostic reagents, controls, calibrators, and laboratory consumables compatible with most major analyzer brands.

Which regions do you serve?

Radiance Healthcare serves customers across India, headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal.

How can I contact Radiance Healthcare?

You can reach us by phone at +91 8777561857, by email at radiancehealthcare2015@gmail.com, via WhatsApp, or through the Contact page. Our team will respond promptly.
